- Start Dates, Start Times, and Completion Dates from different lines are mixed together, making the schedule confusing and unreadable.

- The "All" tab shows separate, distinct tables stacked vertically — one table per line.
- Each table has its own header identifying the line.
- Each table has its own column headers.
