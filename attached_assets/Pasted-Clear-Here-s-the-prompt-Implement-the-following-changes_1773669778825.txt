Identifies which orders should be prioritized based on availability dates, urgency, and current position.

**Example:**
```

schedule to ensure it completes on time. Based on

March 14, 8:00 AM at the latest.
```

**Logic:**
- Scan all active orders on this line.
- Find orders where the estimated completion date is CLOSE to or PAST the availability date.
- Calculate the latest possible start position to meet the deadline.
- Suggest moving the order to a higher Prio.
- Reference specific Prio numbers and order details.
