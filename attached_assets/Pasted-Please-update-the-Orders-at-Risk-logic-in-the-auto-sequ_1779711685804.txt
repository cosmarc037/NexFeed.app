### New at-risk condition
An order is at risk if:
- it is projected to miss its `target_avail_date`
- **or**
- its projected completion time exceeds **5:00 PM**

### Expected result
Orders that are projected to finish after **5:00 PM** should be counted in **Orders at Risk**, even if they are not otherwise late by avail date.

## 4. DEFINE THE 5:00 PM THRESHOLD CLEARLY

Please treat an order as at risk when its projected completion time is:

- **later than 5:00 PM**

### Rule
- `Projected Completion Time <= 5:00 PM` → not at risk under this specific rule
- `Projected Completion Time > 5:00 PM` → at risk under this specific rule

### Example
- `4:45 PM` → not at risk due to the 5:00 PM rule
- `5:00 PM` → not at risk due to the 5:00 PM rule
- `5:01 PM` → at risk
- `6:20 PM` → at risk

### Expected result
The cutoff is strictly **beyond 5:00 PM**, not equal to 5:00 PM.
