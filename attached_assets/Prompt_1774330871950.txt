### 6. ON "ADD ORDER" CLICK — INSERTION BEHAVIOR

**Step 1:** Create the new order with all the filled-in details.

**Step 2:** Determine insertion position:
- If avail date is a specific date → insert at the correct chronological position among dated orders
- If avail date is "prio replenish" or "safety stocks" → insert at the bottom of active orders (above Done/Cancelled), below all dated orders
- If the order has a matching N10D target date → the auto-sequence can later reposition it

**Step 3:** Recalculate cascade:
- All downstream orders' completion dates recascade

**Step 4:** Renumber Prio:
- All active orders renumber from 1

**Step 5:** Show success toast:
```
✅ Order Added

Elite XP Growex 2 (32 MT) has been added to Line 1 
at Prio 3. Downstream orders have been shifted.
```

**Step 6:** Close the dialog.

**Step 7:** Scroll the table to the newly inserted order and briefly highlight it (yellow flash, 2 seconds).
