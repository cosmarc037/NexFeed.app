## 7. DEFINE THE FAITHFULNESS SCORE

Faithfulness Score should be a numeric score from **0 to 100**.

### High-level interpretation:
- **90–100** = strategy is strongly reflected in the final table
- **70–89** = strategy is mostly reflected
- **50–69** = weakly reflected
- **below 50** = poorly reflected / card and preview are misaligned

---

## 8. FAITHFULNESS SCORING DIMENSIONS

Use weighted components based on the strategy’s declared objective.

### Core scoring dimensions:
1. **Intent alignment** (40%)
   - Does the final sequence visibly reflect the primary goal?

2. **Date behavior alignment** (20%)
   - Do actual suggested/applied dates reflect the date strategy?

3. **Grouping behavior alignment** (20%)
   - Does the final order arrangement reflect the stated grouping/compression objective?

4. **Difference from Standard** (10%)
   - Is the final sequence meaningfully different from Standard in the intended way?

5. **Constraint-preserving execution quality** (10%)
   - Was the strategy applied without violating hard rules?

### Example formula:
```javascript
faithfulnessScore =
  intentAlignment * 0.40 +
  dateAlignment * 0.20 +
  groupingAlignment * 0.20 +
  differenceFromStandardScore * 0.10 +
  constraintExecutionScore * 0.10;
```

Round to 1 decimal or integer as needed.
