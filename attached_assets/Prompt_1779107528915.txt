## CELL VALUES

For the monthly matrix view, display the **monthly percentage share of annual demand** in each month cell instead of raw MT.

### Example
If annual total demand for a SKU is 1,674.1 MT, and January demand is 139.9 MT:

January cell should display:
- `8.4%`

### Logic
For each SKU:
- calculate total annual demand
- calculate each month’s share of that annual total
- display that percentage in the cell

---

## TOOLTIP / HOVER DETAIL

If easy to implement, when hovering a month cell, show:
- raw monthly demand in MT
- monthly percentage share

Example tooltip:
- `139.9 MT (8.4% of annual demand)`

This preserves exact values without cluttering the table.

## CONDITIONAL FORMATTING

Please apply conditional formatting / heatmap styling to the monthly cells so users can quickly identify:
- peak months
- average months
- low months

Recommended behavior:
- lower percentages = lighter shade
- higher percentages = darker shade

A single-color heatmap is preferred for simplicity, but a low-to-high gradient is acceptable.

The goal is to make seasonality visually understandable at a glance.
