## 1. REVISE THE 5:00 PM ALLOWANCE RULE FOR “ORDERS AT RISK”

The current 5:00 PM allowance logic appears to be too broad.

Right now, it may be counting orders as at risk even when:
- their projected completion is on a future day
- or their Avail Date is still in the future
- even though they are not actually in same-day deadline pressure

Please refine the rule.

### New intended business rule
An order should be considered **at risk** under the **5:00 PM allowance rule** only if:

- it is already the order’s **Avail Date**
- and the order’s projected completion time is **later than 5:00 PM**

### Expected result
The 5:00 PM risk rule should only apply to orders that are due **today**, not to orders whose Avail Date is still in the future.

---

## 2. DO NOT COUNT FUTURE-DATED ORDERS UNDER THE 5:00 PM RULE

Please make sure the app does **not** count an order as at risk under the 5:00 PM rule just because its estimated completion time is on a later date/time that happens to be after 5:00 PM.

### Example of what should NOT happen
If an order has:
- `Avail Date = May 28`
- `Estimated Completion = May 26, 2026, 4:25 AM`

that order should **not** be counted as at risk under the 5:00 PM allowance rule, because:
- it is **not yet** the order’s Avail Date
- there is no same-day 5:00 PM deadline pressure yet

The same applies to future-dated orders such as:
- Avail Date = `June 5`
- Estimated Completion = `May 26, 2026, 8:54 AM`
- Avail Date = `June 5`
- Estimated Completion = `May 26, 2026, 1:06 PM`

These should **not** be treated as 5:00 PM risk cases.

### Expected result
Future-dated orders are excluded from the 5:00 PM allowance risk condition.

---

## 3. APPLY THE 5:00 PM RULE ONLY WHEN AVAIL DATE = TODAY

Please use this refined rule:

### At-risk under 5:00 PM allowance if:
- `Order Avail Date = Today`
- and `Projected Completion Time > 5:00 PM`

### Not at risk under this rule if:
- `Order Avail Date > Today`
- even if the projected completion time is later than 5:00 PM on some earlier or different date
- unless the order is separately at risk for other existing reasons such as deadline miss or Critical status

### Expected result
The 5:00 PM allowance condition should behave as a **same-day due-date pressure rule**.

---

- the **current date**
- the projected completion time from the sequencing/timing simulation

### Intended logic
If:
- `Avail Date = Today`

then:
- mark it as at risk

then:
- the order should be considered **at risk**

because it is already due today and will exceed the 5:00 PM allowance time.
