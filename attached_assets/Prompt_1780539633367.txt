Please update the **Auto-Sequence AI** so that the **AI itself sorts the orders**, and so that **effective / suggested date** is treated as a **core ranking factor** in the AI’s ordering decision.

---

## 1. MAKE THE AI ITSELF SORT THE ORDERS

Please make the AI responsible for sorting the orders, not just grouping or narrating them.

### Required behavior
The AI should return a ranked sequence that already represents the intended final order of the orders.

### Goal
The AI rank should be the actual basis for the sequence, not just a loose suggestion.

### Expected result
The visible order in the preview should come directly from the AI-ranked output.

---

## 2. TREAT EFFECTIVE / SUGGESTED DATE AS A CORE RANKING FACTOR

Please make sure the AI gives **effective / suggested date** the same importance as the other major sequencing dimensions.

### Important
The AI should not treat date as secondary or optional.

### The AI should consider, together:
- effective / suggested production date
- future-dispatch urgency / status
- changeover impact
- material / category / form / diameter grouping
- production flow efficiency
- other existing sequencing context already provided

### Goal
The AI rank should naturally reflect the correct date-aware order, not just material grouping or changeover optimization.

### Expected result
Orders with earlier effective/suggested dates should generally be ranked earlier unless the AI has a strong reason otherwise.

---

## 3. USE THE EFFECTIVE / SUGGESTED DATE, NOT JUST THE RAW AVAIL DATE

Because the AI may adjust the intended production timing based on order status (for example MTS flexibility), please make the AI rank using the **effective / suggested date** of the order.

### Important
Do not rely only on the original raw avail date.

### The AI should consider:
- original avail date
- urgency / dispatch status
- whether the order can be advanced early
- whether it can be delayed within its allowed window
- and then determine the effective / suggested production date

### Goal
The final rank should reflect the actual intended production timing, not just the original date field.

### Expected result
The AI sorts using the date it actually thinks the order should be produced.

---

## 4. MAKE FUTURE-DISPATCH STATUS A CORE PART OF DATE-AWARE RANKING

Please make sure the AI uses the order’s future-dispatch status in deciding both:
- the effective / suggested date
- and the final rank

### Required interpretation
- **Critical** → should be treated as immediate / today-priority where applicable
- **Urgent** → should be prioritized early
- **Monitor** → can be advanced proactively
- **Sufficient / Flexible / MTS** → may move within the broader allowed planning window

### Goal
The AI should understand how status affects timing and then rank accordingly.

### Expected result
The final AI rank reflects both urgency and date logic.

---

## 5. MAKE THE AI RANK DATE-AWARE AND NOT ONLY CHANGEOVER-AWARE

One of the current problems is that the AI sometimes over-prioritizes:
- grouping
- changeover minimization
- material clustering

while not fully respecting date ordering.

### Required improvement
The AI should balance:
- date-aware ranking
- urgency-aware ranking
- changeover/material grouping

instead of letting grouping dominate the order.

### Goal
The AI should sequence intelligently without visibly breaking the intended date order.

### Expected result
The final order should feel both:
- operationally optimized
- and chronologically sensible

---

## 6. USE THE AI’S RANK AS THE BASIS FOR FINAL SORTING

Once the AI has assigned the final date-aware rank, please use that rank directly as the basis for:
- preview order
- final applied order
- row sequence shown to users

### Goal
Avoid disconnect between:
- AI reasoning
- visible table order
- applied sequence

### Expected result
The final row order should directly reflect the AI rank.

---

## 7. ENSURE ALL ORDERS ARE INCLUDED IN THE AI’S RANKED OUTPUT

Please require the AI to return a ranked result for every input order/entity.

### Goal
Prevent missing rows from falling outside the AI’s actual sequencing logic.

### Expected result
The AI output is complete, and every order participates in the rank-based sort.

---

## IMPORTANT

Please make the **AI itself sort the orders**, and ensure that **effective / suggested date** is treated as a **core ranking factor**.

This means:
- the AI should determine or use the effective/suggested production date
- the AI should use that date in ranking the orders
- the AI should balance date, urgency, grouping, and changeover
- the AI’s returned rank should be the basis for the final sequence shown in the preview and apply flow

The goal is for the AI to produce a rank that is already:
- date-aware
- urgency-aware
- sequencing-aware
- and suitable to use directly as the order of the rows
