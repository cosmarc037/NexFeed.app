Please revisit the **Auto-Sequence AI** so that it has stronger context on **production time**, **changeover time**, and **estimated completion timing** when deciding both the **sequence** and the **suggested date**.

Right now, there are still cases where the AI suggests a date that does not seem consistent with the actual runtime of the orders and the resulting completion timing.

---

## 1. GIVE THE AI EXPLICIT CONTEXT ON PRODUCTION TIME PER ORDER

Please make sure the AI receives each order’s production time as a first-class input.

### This should include
- calculated production time
- run rate basis
- total duration needed for the order

### Goal
The AI should understand how long each order will actually take to produce before assigning its sequence position or suggested date.

### Expected result
The AI can make more realistic sequence and date decisions based on actual order duration.

---

## 2. GIVE THE AI EXPLICIT CONTEXT ON CHANGEOVER TIME

Please make sure the AI receives the expected changeover time between orders as part of the sequencing context.

### This should include
- changeover impact from the previous/adjacent order
- category / color / diameter / form transitions where relevant
- total changeover burden affecting the schedule

### Goal
The AI should understand that the real time consumed is not just production time, but also changeover time.

### Expected result
The AI can better judge whether a date/position is realistic once changeovers are considered.

---

## 3. LET THE AI REASON ABOUT ESTIMATED COMPLETION DATE / TIME

Please improve the AI context so it can reason not just about the order’s suggested date, but also about when the order would actually complete based on:
- production time
- changeover time
- sequence placement
- cumulative load of the orders ahead of it

### Goal
The AI should be able to recognize when an order placed on a given date would actually finish too late.

### Expected result
Suggested dates become more realistic because the AI understands the likely completion timing.

---

## 4. MAKE THE AI USE PRODUCTION TIME + CHANGEOVER + CUMULATIVE LOAD TO DECIDE SUGGESTED DATE

Please improve the AI so that its suggested date is based not only on strategy or urgency, but also on:
- how long the order takes
- what changeover time is needed
- what work is already scheduled ahead of it
- and when it would realistically complete

### Goal
Avoid cases where an order gets a suggested date that looks too early or too optimistic compared to its estimated completion date.

### Expected result
Suggested date and estimated completion timing become more aligned.

---

## 5. DO NOT LET THE AI SUGGEST A DATE WITHOUT UNDERSTANDING WHETHER THE ORDER CAN REALISTICALLY FINISH AROUND THAT DATE

There are cases where:
- the AI gives an order a suggested date
- but the actual estimated completion spills too far or creates delay risk

### Goal
The AI should not suggest a date blindly. It should know whether the order can realistically be completed in the intended time window.

### Expected result
The AI should assign a later date when production time + changeover + queue length make the earlier date unrealistic.

---

## 6. MAKE THE AI MORE AWARE OF `DELAY RISK` WHEN CHOOSING SUGGESTED DATE

If the order’s estimated completion timing suggests delay risk, the AI should account for that during the sequencing decision itself.

### Goal
Suggested date should be informed by the risk of late completion, not just by strategic grouping.

### Expected result
The AI should place or date long-running orders more realistically to reduce delay-risk situations.

---

## 7. KEEP THIS CONTEXT ACTIVE FOR BOTH TOP-PRIORITY AND FLEXIBLE ORDERS

This improvement should apply to:
- Critical orders
- MTO orders
- Monitor/Urgent orders
- Flexible / MTS orders

### Goal
All order types should benefit from more realistic runtime-aware date assignment.

### Expected result
The AI becomes more operationally realistic across the whole sequence.

---

## IMPORTANT

Please improve Auto-Sequence so that the AI has explicit context on:

- **production time**
- **changeover time**
- **estimated completion timing**
- **cumulative load from earlier orders**

and uses those to decide:
- the sequence position
- the suggested date

The AI should not suggest a date without understanding whether the order can realistically complete around that date.

This is needed because there are still cases where the suggested date does not appear aligned with the runtime and completion reality of the order.
